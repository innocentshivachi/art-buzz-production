The Production

A stage for Murang'a's set books.

Art Buzz Production is a prolific environment filled with visionaries holding holistic potential across the performing arts — poetry, live stage performances, script writing, plays, stand-up comedy, spoken word and more.

Based in Murang'a, the production specialises in live performances of set books, staged for audiences from Junior Secondary Schools right through to Senior Secondary Schools — turning the literature on the syllabus into performance students remember.

Art Buzz Production has become a source of inspiration to youth within the vicinity of Murang'a University of Technology, and to university students and the wider community at large.

Executive Producer

Allan Wafula

Known to Murang'a as "Pro Wafula"

Allan Wafula owns and produces Art Buzz Production. A visionary who brought life to art in Murang'a, he left a lasting impression as the first to introduce Set-Book productions to the region.

"Aspire to inspire generational talents."

Under his direction, Art Buzz Production continues to give young people in and around Murang'a University of Technology a stage to discover what performing arts can be.
